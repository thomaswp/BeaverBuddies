v1.7.1
* A changelog will now appear on version updates (that's this window!).
* New Feature: Players can now ping the map. Open Settings->Keybindings and choose a binding for "Ping Location" to ping in game and all players will see it. Credit: @SamuZad
* You can change the player name and color associated with your ping under Mods->BeaverBuddies (settings button).
* New Setting: "Reduce the number of forced pauses" stops the game from auto-pausing when deconstructing/researching, or optionally when opening the menu. Change settings with Mods->BeaverBuddies (settings button). Credit: @SamuZad
* Desync fixes:
  * Setting Automatable inputs to (None)
  * Power Clutch settings. Credit: @JoeStead
  * Badwater Discharge automation Credit: @JoeStead
* Placing district centers and connections should cause fewer crashes and desyncs (some may still occur, so consider saving beforehand). Credit: @SamuZad
